Random Facts About Space

Did you know that the universe is constantly expanding and is currently expected to expand until everything is so far apart, it effectively dies due to a lack of heat? Another theory expects the universe to eventually stop expanding and start collapsing back in on itself.

Did you know that not only is Earth rotating around our Sun, but that our Sun is moving our Solar System around a black hole at the center of the Milky Way, and that the Milky Way is moving across space as well? Lots of moving parts!

Did you know that the Sun produces a noise? We can't hear it since sound waves require a medium to move through, and there's a void between us, but NASA made a recording of it!
